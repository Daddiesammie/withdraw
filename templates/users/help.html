Frequently Asked Questions

Why do I need to complete all verification steps?

Our multi-step verification process ensures maximum security for your funds and complies with international financial regulations.

How long does the verification process take?

Each verification step is typically processed within 24 hours. The entire process can be completed within 2-3 business days.

What documents are required for ID verification?

We accept valid passport, driver's license, or national ID card. Documents must be clear, current, and unedited.

How do I receive my withdrawal OTP?

The OTP is sent to your registered email address or phone number for enhanced security.
